Our Mission

Empire State Greenhouses, LLC (ESG LLC), a subsidiary of Chobe Advisers, LLC, is developing a high impact closed loop energy & agriculture project with an Environmental, Social & Governance approach. ESG LLC is poised to develop a resilient food system for the future by building sustainable farming operations incorporating a carbon-negative footprint and organic, locally grown solutions with an emphasis on enhanced security for the food system and supply chain.
