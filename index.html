Empire State Greenhouses, LLC (ESG), a subsidiary of Chobe Advisers, LLC, is developing a high impact-closed loop energy & agriculture project with an Environmental, Social & Governance approach.

Our Mission

ESG is poised to develop a resilient food system for the future by building sustainable farming operations incorporating a carbon-negative footprint and organic, locally grown solutions. Our emphesis is on enhanced security for the US food system and supply chain. Concurrently, ESG's energy system will operate on solar, geothermal, and biogas. Our biogas digester will porvide additional energy for our complex and the surrounding area while providing a carbon reduction solution to local dairy farmers in New York state. This high impact,closed-loop energy and agriculture project will serve environmental, social, and food security goals.
